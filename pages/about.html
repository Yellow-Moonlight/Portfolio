Seongjoo Moon is a media artist who explores the relationship between humans and technology across the boundaries of art and engineering. Seongjoo Moon's practice is based on various technical media, including web programming, physical computing, sound and video art, 3D modeling, and digital fabrication.

Currently, Moon focuses on developing toy instruments for children, seeking new forms of artistic experience that integrate play, learning, and technological creativity.
