On My Head(workshop)

Participants will take part in a workshop where they purchase materials from the Dongdaemun Garment Market to decorate hats based on a selected theme. Each participant will work within a budget of 10,000 won (approximately €7), create their hat, and later share and discuss their creations with the group.
