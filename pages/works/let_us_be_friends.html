Let's be freinds

An interactive wooden device uses Arduino and sensors to physically press a camera’s shutter when someone passes by, inviting spontaneous connection between human and machine.

2014

"Let’s Be Friends" is an interactive installation that explores the relationship between humans and machines through a simple yet poetic gesture. A wooden mechanism equipped with Arduino and motion sensors detects when someone walks in front of a camera. In response, it physically presses the camera’s shutter button, capturing the moment automatically.
